## Key RCM Optimization Steps
To ensure financial health in 2026, practices must focus on these three pillars:
 
*   **✅ Patient Pre-Registration:** Verify insurance eligibility at least 48 hours prior to service to prevent front-end denials.
*   **✅ Coding Accuracy:** Conduct annual reviews of ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS updates to stay compliant with 2026 standards.
*   **✅ Denial Management:** Implement a monthly tracking system for your top 5 denial reasons to identify and fix root causes.
